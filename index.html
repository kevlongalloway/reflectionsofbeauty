Robin Durr, FNP-BC

Chief Executive Officer, Clinician

Leadership

Chief Executive
Officer, Clinician

Robin Durr, MSN, FNP-BC, is an esteemed healthcare professional with 30 years of experience as a Registered Nurse and 27 years as a Family Nurse Practitioner. Her expertise spans various healthcare settings, including Geriatrics, Pediatric Primary Care, Emergency Medicine, Research, and Military Health Care. However, her true passion lies in the field of Aesthetic Medicine.

In 2008, Robin served as the Director of Nursing for Reveal Med Spa. In this role, she oversaw nursing programs across seven centers in three states. Her responsibilities included training nurses, performing procedures herself, and ensuring staffing initiatives and policy development were executed effectively.

Throughout her career journey thus far, Robin has received numerous awards for her outstanding contributions to multiple organizations. She remains committed to providing world-class patient care while embracing the unique beauty within each individual.

Dedicated to enhancing patient care experiences through innovative approaches that celebrate uniqueness and empower individuals to embrace their inner beauty, Robin strives for excellence in service delivery operations by optimizing workforce performance and utilizing best practices.

As Owner/CEO of Reflections of Beauty, Robin Durr continues to transform standards within the field of Aesthetics by prioritizing exceptional patient care above all else — ensuring every client leaves feeling empowered by their own unique allure.
